Privacy Policy

Kanji Yokai  ·  Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information Kanji Yokai collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

What We Collect

Kanji Yokai stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Study progress (kanji learned, SRS review schedule)
  • Collection data (yokai cards earned)
  • App preferences (theme selection, settings)

This data never leaves your device. We do not operate any servers or databases that store your personal information.

Payments

All purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through In-App Purchases. We never see, store, or have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all payment data: apple.com/legal/privacy.

Analytics & Tracking

Kanji Yokai does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not track you across apps or websites.

Children

Kanji Yokai does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. All data is stored locally on the device and no information is transmitted to external servers.

Data Retention & Deletion

All app data is stored on your device. To delete all data, simply delete the app. If you use iCloud backup, your device backup may include app data — you can manage this in iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage.

Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Because we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no data for us to provide, correct, or delete on request. Your data lives on your device and is entirely under your control.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at: hello@kanjiyokai.com
